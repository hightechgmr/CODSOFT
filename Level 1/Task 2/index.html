About Me

Hi, I'm Harshit Pathak — a curious and creative developer with a passion for web design and game development. I enjoy transforming ideas into clean, functional digital experiences that are both engaging and efficient.
With a solid foundation in HTML and CSS, and a growing interest in modern frameworks and tools, I'm constantly experimenting — whether it's designing portfolios, building interactive interfaces, or prototyping game logic.

More About Me

When I’m not coding or designing, I’m usually diving into stories, visuals, and experiences that inspire me. Whether it’s through movies, video games, or just exploring new ideas, I love observing how creativity and emotion come together.
I’ve always been drawn to interactive worlds — how they’re built, how they feel, and how they connect with people. That’s a big reason why I enjoy game development and designing user experiences that are not just functional, but memorable.
For me, inspiration can come from anywhere — a cinematic scene, a great game mechanic, or even a simple conversation. I believe great digital experiences start with good stories, and I’m excited to keep learning how to build those, one project at a time.
